I really like this flavor of GT kombucha. It conjures up wonderful memories of eating entire pomelos during Lunar New Year. However, it is pretty acidic and gets rather sour the longer you drink it. I still buy it because I sometimes crave a sour beverage; I would not have this as my first sip of kombucha ever.
